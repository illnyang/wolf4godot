Wolfenstein 3D Reimagined

Project Overview

Faithful Recreation

Every detail of the original Wolfenstein 3D has been carefully recreated — from enemy AI patterns to authentic sound effects and pixel-perfect graphics.

Fully Moddable

Built with extensibility in mind. Create custom levels, modify gameplay mechanics, and add new content using Godot's powerful tools.

Cross-Platform

Play on Windows, Linux, macOS, and even in your browser. Godot Engine ensures smooth performance across all major platforms.

Open Source

Completely open-source and free. Explore the code, learn from it, contribute improvements, or use it as a foundation for your own projects.
